The Trouble with Bears

By Evan Wild

Tall, lumbering, angry, dangerous. The real live bears of this world are proud, independent creatures...

So what are bears really like, and why does the world's media portray them with such a skewed vision?

Types of Bear

Bears come in two varieties — large and medium. You don't get small bears...

The table below gives you some useful facts about bears:

Bear types and their characteristics
Bear Type Coat Adult size Habitat Lifespan Diet
Wild Brown or black 1.4 to 2.8 meters Woods and forests 25 to 28 years Fish, meat, plants
Urban North Face 18 to 22 Condos and coffee shops 20 to 32 years Starbucks, sushi

Habitats and Eating Habits

Wild bears eat a variety of meat, fish, fruit, nuts, and other naturally growing ingredients...

Urban bears scavenge from dumpsters, bins, and fast food joints. They sleep in various urban places...
